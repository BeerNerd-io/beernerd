About Beer Nerd

At Beer Nerd, we craft both beer and technology to help people, communities, and the fun of it.

Building Cool Stuff

We're passionate about creating cutting-edge tools and applications that push the boundaries of what's possible. We make craft beer and craft tools for humans, communities, and most importantly, for fun. Please reach out to info@beernerd.io for inquiries.

Learning and Growing

We believe in continuous learning and improvement. Every project is an opportunity to expand our knowledge, refine our skills, and share our discoveries with the community.

Head Nerd: Jeff St. Andre

Jeff is the head nerd behind Beer Nerd, combining his passion for craft beer with his expertise in technology. With over a decade of experience in educational technology, home brewing, and leading interactive and educational craft beer tours with City Brew Tours Austin, Jeff brings a unique perspective to the worlds of both beer and technology. When he's not coding or brewing, you can find him exploring new breweries or spending time with his family.
